What is litter-finder all about?

We know how essential cleanliness is, it is perhaps a basic necessity which every individual demands to have."litter-finder" is an intiative to use technology in order to find areas which are affected with scattered garbage and to track those areas down until they're prefectly cleaned. We here believe that through litter-finder we eliminate all such places and thereby make this world a better,cleaner place
